Indoor gardening is a year-round delight, and your plants' needs change with the seasons. To ensure your indoor garden remains healthy and vibrant, follow this seasonal plant care guide. Learn how to adapt your care routine as the seasons change and create a thriving indoor garden throughout the year.

Spring Renewal

As spring brings new growth and energy, your plants may need repotting and fresh nutrients. Discover essential spring care tasks to help your indoor garden thrive during this season of renewal.

Summer Sunshine

With summer's bright sunshine, your plants may require more frequent watering and protection from the heat. Find tips for maintaining proper moisture levels and protecting your indoor garden during the summer months.

Autumn Transition

Autumn brings cooler temperatures and less daylight. Learn how to prepare your plants for the transition by adjusting watering schedules and providing adequate lighting. This is the time to bring hardy outdoor plants back indoors.

Winter Coziness

During the winter, your indoor garden may experience lower humidity and reduced light. Explore strategies for keeping your plants cozy and thriving through the colder months, including humidification and supplemental lighting.

Your Year-Round Indoor Garden

Maintaining your indoor garden year-round is an enjoyable and rewarding experience. For guidance tailored to each season, consider using the Florra plant care and management app. It provides seasonal care recommendations to keep your plants healthy, whatever the weather.
